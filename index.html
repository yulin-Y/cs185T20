3B(Bouldering Beta Buddy)

We want to make climbing improvement feel more accessible by helping users find useful examples from others, upload their own attempts, and receive personalized AI coaching that fits how they actually climb in busy gyms.

What we can do

Discover New Beta

Explore climbing videos and route ideas from other climbers. Browse different approaches, compare techniques, and find inspiration for your next attempt.

Capture Your Climbs

Upload your own climbing videos to save attempts, review your movement, and build a record of your progress over time.

Receive Smart Coaching

Get personalized AI feedback based on your climbs. Learn what to adjust, what worked well, and how to improve more effectively.

Build Confidence and Progress

Turn every session into a learning opportunity. 3B helps you reflect, improve, and become a stronger, more confident climber.
